January 28, 2020

Travel day

On this day my birthday a perfect day with a breakfast watching the horses in the meadow, going to see the long-abandoned plane, visiting the waterfalls, eating at the farm famous for its ice creams and going to the Blue Laggon, a fantasy of place with hot springs and beautiful views, watching the geysers jumping into the sky and to end the day have dinner at a restaurant in the city, La casa Nordica, a spectacular trip in every way.

January 26, 2020

I'm going to Iceland!

My trip started two days before my birthday, catching a flight at seven in the morning to Iceland. Arriving at 11 in the morning in Iceland, admiring the views from the window of the completely white ground covered in snow, enjoying its food and its fresh air. With a lot of horses while you were on the road. An impressive day and at the end of the day a hotel with breathtaking views of the sky and the best thing at dawn to see the northern lights.
